Latitude and Climate, what does one have to do with the other?

Using data from OpenWeatherMap's API, we take a look at random samples of weather data for precipitation, temperature, cloudiness and wind speeds for 455 cities spread over both Northern and Southern Hemispheres. We then determine whether there is a correlation between climate variables and latitudes.
