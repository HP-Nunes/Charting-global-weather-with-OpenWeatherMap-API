An Analytical Observation of the Relationship Between Weather and Latitude

Using data from OpenWeatherMap's API, we take a look at weather data for humidity, temperature, cloudiness and wind speeds for 455 randomly selected cities spread over Northern and Southern Hemispheres. We then determine whether there is a correlation between climate variables and latitudes.
